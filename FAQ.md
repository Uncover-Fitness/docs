## General

**What is Uncover?**
Uncover is a unified training and client management platform for fitness studios, trainers, nutritionists, and their clients. It combines class booking, workout programming, nutrition tracking, AI coaching, body composition scanning, heart rate monitoring, and recovery session management into one seamless system — available on web, iOS, and Android.

**Who is Uncover for?**
Uncover serves fitness studios, personal trainers, nutritionists, corporate wellness programs, and their clients. Whether you're a single trainer with a growing client base or a multi-location studio, Uncover scales with you.

**What platforms does Uncover support?**
Uncover is available as a web dashboard (for admins, trainers, and clients), an iOS app, and an Android app.

## Wearables & Devices

**Which wearable devices does Uncover support?**
Uncover integrates with Apple Watch (via HealthKit), Garmin, WHOOP, Oura, Polar (H10 heart rate monitor), Fitbit, and Withings. We pull data from all of them into one unified view.

**Do I need a specific wearable to use Uncover?**
No. Wearable integration is optional. You can use Uncover without any connected device — manual logging works for all features. Wearables simply add automatic data collection.

**Can I switch wearable devices without losing my data?**
Yes. Your biometric history is stored in Uncover, not on the device. Switch devices whenever you want.

---

## AI Features

**What does the AI Coach do?**
The AI Coach provides personalized coaching through chat and voice conversations. It has access to your training history, body composition data, biometric trends, and diagnostic reports — so its recommendations are specific to you, not generic.

**Is the AI Coach a replacement for my trainer?**
No. The AI Coach is designed to amplify your trainer, not replace them. It handles quick questions, daily planning, and health summaries between sessions. For clinical concerns or complex coaching decisions, it directs you to your trainer.

**How does the food scanner work?**
Take a photo of your meal and the AI will identify the food items and estimate calories, protein, carbs, and fat. You can adjust the results before logging.

## Privacy & Security

**How is my health data protected?**
Your health data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use role-based access control, JWT authentication with MFA support, and HIPAA-aligned infrastructure. Access is audited.

**Do you sell my data?**
No. Never. Your health data belongs to you.

**Can I delete my data?**
Yes. You can request a full data export or deletion at any time. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

**Can I control what data is collected?**
Yes. Health data consent is explicit and withdrawable. You choose which wearable integrations to enable or disable.

## For Studios & Trainers

**Can I manage multiple locations?**
Yes. Uncover supports multi-studio architecture with studio boundary middleware. Each location can have its own classes, staff, and settings while sharing a unified management layer.

**Does Uncover handle payments?**
Yes. Uncover integrates with Stripe for membership subscriptions, credit packages, add-on services, and invoicing.

**Can I track staff payroll and commissions?**
Yes. The admin portal includes time and attendance, shift scheduling, payroll period exports (CSV and QuickBooks compatible), and configurable commission tracking (per-session, per-class, or percentage-based).
