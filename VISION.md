## The Problem

The fitness industry is fragmented. A single studio or personal trainer juggles five to ten disconnected tools — one for scheduling, another for payments, a separate app for workout programming, a spreadsheet for nutrition plans, and a handful of wearable dashboards that never talk to each other.

Clients feel it too. Their workout data lives in one app, nutrition in another, heart rate data on their watch, and body composition results in a PDF they'll never open again. There's no single place that connects the dots between what they eat, how they train, how they recover, and how they perform.

The result? Trainers spend more time on admin than coaching. Clients lose motivation because their progress is scattered across platforms. Studios can't see the full picture of their members' health journeys. And the people who need the most support — those managing chronic conditions, recovering from injury, or just starting their fitness journey — are underserved by tools designed for elite athletes.

## Our Mission

> To empower individuals and organizations with the data, tools, and coaching they need to optimize human performance, accelerate recovery, and build lasting health habits.

We believe that clinical-grade health intelligence shouldn't be reserved for professional athletes and luxury wellness retreats. Every person who walks into a studio, logs a meal, or straps on a heart rate monitor deserves a platform that makes sense of their data and helps them improve.

## Our Vision

> A world where every person has access to clinical-grade health intelligence — to make informed decisions about their performance, recovery, and longevity.

## What We Believe

### Technology amplifies the coach — it never replaces the human relationship

AI is powerful. But the moment a client shares a health concern, struggles with motivation, or needs someone to believe in them — that's a human moment. Our AI Coach is trained to recognize when to redirect to a real trainer. Our reports empower coaches with data and context so they can have better conversations, not fewer.

### Recovery is performance

Rest days aren't wasted days. Cryotherapy, infrared sauna, compression therapy, and sleep optimization aren't luxuries — they're part of the training methodology. Uncover treats recovery as a first-class service, not an afterthought.

### What gets measured gets improved

A single workout log tells you very little. But 14 days of heart rate data, sleep patterns, nutrition logs, and training sessions — connected and analyzed together — reveal the story behind your progress. Uncover tracks behavior in 14-day cycles, surfacing trends that help trainers and clients make smarter decisions.

### Your data belongs to you

Health data is deeply personal. We never sell it. Members can see exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how to delete it. Consent is withdrawable. Transparency is non-negotiable.

### Inclusive by design

We score performance relative to your context — age, occupation, fitness level, and goals. A warehouse worker and a desk worker have different baseline demands. A new mother and a competitive athlete have different definitions of progress. Uncover adapts, because excellence looks different for everyone.

## What Makes Uncover Different

### One platform, not ten tools

Uncover replaces the patchwork of scheduling apps, payment processors, workout trackers, nutrition logs, wearable dashboards, and messaging tools with a single cohesive system. Trainers manage everything from one portal. Clients see everything in one app.

### AI coaching grounded in real data

Our AI Coach doesn't give generic advice. It reads your diagnostic reports, movement screenings, body composition scans, and biometric history. When it suggests skipping heavy legs today, it's because your recovery metrics say so — not because of a generic algorithm.

### Wearable aggregation, not wearable lock-in

Apple Watch, Garmin, WHOOP, Oura, Polar, Fitbit, Withings — Uncover pulls data from them all into one unified view. Switch devices whenever you want. Your data history doesn't reset.

### Studio meets digital

Book a cryotherapy session, attend a HIIT class with live heart rate tracking on the studio leaderboard, then review your AI-generated recovery recommendations at home. The physical and digital experiences are seamlessly connected.

### Enterprise-ready from day one

Multi-studio architecture. Role-based access for admins, trainers, nutritionists, and clients. Staff scheduling, payroll exports, commission tracking, and compliance reporting. Built for organizations that take health seriously.

## The Platform

Uncover is a full-stack ecosystem spanning three surfaces:

**Web Dashboard** — A multi-portal system for admins, trainers, nutritionists, and clients. Manage classes, programs, clients, staff, payments, and analytics from one place.

**iOS & Android Apps** — Native mobile apps with voice-coached workouts, real-time heart rate monitoring, 3D body composition scanning, nutrition tracking, AI coaching, and class booking.

**AI Engine** — Powered by Gemini, our AI layer handles personalized coaching conversations, food photo analysis, body scan interpretation, workout plan generation, and clinical report narratives.

### Key Capabilities

| Area | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| **Class Booking** | Browse timetables, book sessions, join waitlists, get reminders |
| **Heart Rate Tracking** | Real-time BPM and zone tracking during class via Polar H10, with studio leaderboards |
| **Body Composition** | 3D scan analysis — body fat, lean mass, BMI, BMR, measurements over time |
| **Workout Programs** | Custom trainer-designed programs with video demos, set/rep logging, PRs |
| **Nutrition** | Meal logging, calorie and macro tracking, AI-powered food photo scanning |
| **AI Coach** | Context-aware chat and voice coaching, daily plans, health summaries |
| **Recovery** | Book cryo, infrared sauna, and compression sessions — tracked alongside training |
| **Progress Analytics** | Dashboards, trend charts, achievements, gamification, leaderboards |
| **Clinical Reports** | 7-page diagnostic reports combining biometrics, AI narratives, and movement analysis |
| **Messaging** | Trainer-client conversations, push notifications, email updates |
